NO RETURN: THE FIRST FALL

Chapter 12: In the Depths of Loss

Posted on June 29th, 2023

As the morning sun cast its warm glow over the forest, Nikki and Dalia solemnly stood before a freshly dug grave, their eyes filled with sorrow. They had given Mal a proper burial, paying their respects to their fallen companion. Nikki's heart ached as she realized she was now the last one left of the original three. The weight of their journey and the losses they had endured pressed heavily upon her.

Dalia, ever perceptive, sensed Nikki's melancholy. She disappeared into the depths of the cave and returned with a small bundle of herbs. With skilled hands, she brewed a pot of herbal coffee, hoping to offer some solace to her grieving friend. As they sat by the campfire, the fragrant aroma filled the air, intertwining with their shared memories of a life left behind.

Nikki took a sip of the herbal brew, its warmth enveloping her senses. She felt a flicker of comfort, knowing that despite the hardships, she had found a new ally in Dalia. Their conversation meandered through the stories of their past lives, their dreams, and the loved ones they had left behind. Amidst the flickering firelight, a bond formed between them, a shared understanding of the resilience required to navigate the treacherous path they now tread.

Feeling a renewed sense of purpose, Nikki suggested they venture to the nearby river to collect water. The rhythmic sound of flowing water offered a semblance of tranquility. As they approached the riverbank, their eyes widened with shock. Amongst the dense foliage, Dalia recognized a figure struggling against an assailant dressed in dark clothing. Fear gripped her heart, knowing that it was someone from her previous group, someone she had thought lost forever.

Without a moment's hesitation, Nikki stepped forward, her voice firm and commanding. She demanded the man in black put down his gun, even though he appeared unarmed. A tense standoff ensued, as Nikki's finger tightened around the trigger, her resolve unyielding. With a single shot, she pierced the man's heart, extinguishing his life. Dalia looked on, taken aback by the cold determination in Nikki's eyes.

Breathing heavily, Dalia turned her attention to Nytro, the man they had saved from the clutches of his attacker. His face etched with panic, he revealed that he had been searching tirelessly for his wife and son, desperate to find them. Dalia, filled with compassion, gently reassured him, promising to aid in the search for his missing loved ones. Together, they would navigate the perilous unknown, their bond forged in the crucible of shared loss.

Meanwhile, Nikki, haunted by the act she had just committed, remained behind to dispose of the man's lifeless body. She pondered the futility of their existence in this unforgiving wilderness. The never-ending cycle of survival gnawed at her spirit, casting a shadow of doubt upon their purpose. But deep down, she knew that within the depths of despair, there still flickered a glimmer of hope, a drive to persevere in the face of adversity.

As she made her way back to camp, the weight of her actions settled upon her shoulders. She questioned the true meaning of their existence in this harsh and unforgiving world.

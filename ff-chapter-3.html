NO RETURN: THE FIRST FALL

Chapter Three: A Twist of Fate

Posted on June 16th, 2023

The sun's gentle rays pierced through the canopy, signaling the start of a new day in the rainforest. Nikki and Mal emerged from their slumber, their bodies weary but determined. As they rubbed their bleary eyes, they set about their usual tasks—collecting firewood, gathering edible plants, and tending to their meager camp.

Harris, ever the lone wolf, ventured away from the camp, driven by the necessity to hunt for food. He stalked through the dense foliage, his senses heightened, as he embarked on his first solo expedition. Meanwhile, Nikki and Mal, left alone at the camp, made their way to the nearby river to wash their clothes, finding solace in the tranquil sounds of flowing water.

As they dipped their garments into the cool river, a sense of vulnerability washed over them. Mal broke the silence, her voice filled with a mix of hope and doubt. "Do you think we'll ever make it back home? Or are we destined to be stranded here forever?" she asked, her words laced with uncertainty.

Nikki paused for a moment, her gaze fixed on the flowing river. "I refuse to believe this is the end. We'll find a way. We have to," she replied, determination ringing in her voice. The conversation lingered for a while as they contemplated their uncertain fate, finding solace in each other's presence.

Suddenly, a flickering glow caught their attention. Horror filled their eyes as they realized their makeshift shelter was engulfed in flames. Without hesitation, they dashed towards the blaze, fear driving them to salvage their belongings before they were devoured by the inferno. But to their dismay, their possessions had vanished, leaving nothing but charred remnants behind.

A chill ran down Mal's spine as she surveyed the scene. Something felt off—too orchestrated to be an accident. She turned to Nikki, her voice tinged with concern. "This isn't right. We need to find Harris and figure out what's happening," she urged, her instincts warning her of the lurking danger.

They set off, determined to unravel the mystery that shrouded their camp. However, their pursuit was abruptly halted as a heavy blow struck them from behind. Mal's world faded into darkness as she succumbed to unconsciousness, while Nikki's vision blurred, her head throbbing with pain.

As the haze cleared, Nikki struggled to focus her vision, her surroundings slowly coming into view. Two figures stood over them, their tattered clothes and muck-streaked skin revealing a life of hardship. Fear gripped her heart as she realized they were at the mercy of these enigmatic strangers.

With cautious steps, the two mysterious men loomed over Nikki, their intentions veiled in uncertainty. The weight of the situation bore down upon her, her fate entwined with that of Mal and Harris. Nikki's pulse quickened, her mind racing to comprehend the perilous predicament they found themselves in.
